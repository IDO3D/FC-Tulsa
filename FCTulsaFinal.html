FC Tulsa // Club History & Culture
ORIGINS

Est. 1978

FC Tulsa carries a rich legacy rooted in the heart of Oklahoma. Representing the 918, the club has become a cornerstone of professional soccer in the American Midwest.

Competing in the USL Championship — one of the premier second-division leagues in North America — FC Tulsa plays home matches at ONEOK Field in downtown Tulsa.
